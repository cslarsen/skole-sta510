\problem{1 (a)}
The density function of the \textit{exponential distribution} is given in
\cite{walpole} as
\[
  f(x) =
    \begin{cases}
      \displaystyle
        \frac{1}{\beta} e^{\sfrac{-x}{\beta}} & x > 0  \\
        0 & \text{elsewhere}
    \end{cases}
\]
We then have that
\[
  P(X \leqslant x) = \int_{-\infty}^{x} f(t)\, \mathrm{d}t
\]
